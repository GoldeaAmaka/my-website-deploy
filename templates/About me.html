About Me

This is what I do.

Hello! I’m Ukamaka Scholastica Okechukwu, a passionate Digital Marketing and Data Analysis student at York St John University. I’m driven by the challenge of turning raw data into meaningful insights that inspire smarter marketing decisions and more human digital experiences. My degree has given me a strong foundation in digital strategy, consumer analytics, data visualization, and marketing research, allowing me to combine creativity with evidence-based thinking. I enjoy exploring how marketing data reveals audience behaviour and how ethical, data-driven storytelling can strengthen the relationship between brands and people.

Beyond university, I’m continuously developing my technical and analytical skills — from working with tools like Python, Tableau, and Excel, to experimenting with web and content projects that merge data analysis with creative communication. I’m particularly interested in the future of ethical digital marketing, where data integrity, transparency, and innovation work together to create genuine value for audiences. My long-term goal is to build a career that bridges marketing intelligence, business strategy, and digital transformation — helping organisations grow responsibly through the power of data.

When I’m not studying or coding, I love designing user experiences, analysing campaign performance metrics, and writing about the intersection of data, creativity, and technology.
